About Me

Maryana Manssor

I am a software engineer with a Bachelor's degree in Informatics Engineering from the University of Aleppo, and currently pursuing a Master's degree in Web Sciences at the Virtual University of Syria. I have over three years of experience in front-end web development and Python programming.

My areas of expertise include:

  • Web design using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and Bootstrap.
  • Artificial intelligence development using Python.
  • Web development using Python and Flask.

Throughout my career, I have successfully delivered various web and AI projects. My passion for learning new technologies drives me to continually improve my skills in both front-end and back-end development.

In addition to technical skills, I am also interested in UI/UX design, ensuring that the websites I develop are not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly.
